This project, Nomad Nirvana, was created with a team of developers in the LaunchCode program using Java, Javascript, APIs and MySQL. The purpose of the site was to allow users to plan trips in a convenient way by compiling their trip planning resources (such as inspiration videos, itineraries, etc.) into one place.

Notable features:

  • Ability to create, edit or delete vacations from persistent database
  • Countdown banner on trip view page displaying amount of time until upcoming vacation
  • Users can embed videos from YouTube onto the homepage (shows all videos) and trip view pages (videos are filtered by trip). Future feature: adding logic for Instagram reels as well
  • WIP: Weather widget on vacation view page displays destination weather conditions
